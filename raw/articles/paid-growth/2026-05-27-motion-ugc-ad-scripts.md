## TL;DR

UGC scripts can use direct-response structure without sounding like brand copy. The common pattern is hook -> problem -> solution -> value proposition -> social proof -> CTA, adapted to the creator's voice.

## Key Claims

- UGC scripts need a hook strong enough to stop the scroll in the first seconds.
- The strongest UGC frameworks guide the viewer through pain, solution, value, proof, and action.
- Visual hooks and word hooks can both carry attention.
- The structure should disappear into a believable creator performance.

## Important Details

- Motion identifies visual hooks such as immediate transformations, product reveals, and split-screen comparisons.
- The guide distinguishes the script framework from the finished performance: viewers should not feel a rigid formula.
- UGC works better when written for the creator's natural speech and platform format.
- The guide reinforces sound-first and short-form editing considerations.
