Available on: Mac, Windows, iOS, Android (Beta)Dictate anywhere you can type — 4x faster than typing. Flow transcribes your speech in real time, supports AI commands to edit your text, and adapts to your vocabulary over time.Flow is a voice dictation app that works in any text field on your computer or phone. Press a hotkey, speak naturally, and Flow transcribes your words in real time. It adapts to your voice, learns your vocabulary, and can execute AI commands like "make this more professional" or "summarize this."How it worksOverviewFlow runs in the background and activates when you press a hotkey. Speak naturally and your words appear as text wherever your cursor is — in any app, browser, or text field.Key behaviorsReal-time transcription: Flow converts your speech to text as you speak, with no delay.AI commands: Say instructions like "make this more professional" or "summarize this" and Flow edits your text accordingly.Vocabulary adaptation: Flow learns your words, names, and technical terms over time for more accurate transcription.Notes (iOS): Dictate and save notes directly in the Flow app using the built-in microphone button. Notes are searchable via Spotlight, accessible from a lock screen widget or Control Center button, and can be created hands-free using Siri shortcuts. A quick AI summary is available on each note with a single tap.Personalization Styles (Android): Flow automatically adjusts the tone of your dictation — Formal, Casual, Very Casual, or Excited — based on which app you're typing in. Set a style for app categories like Personal, Work, Email, and Other, and Flow applies the right tone automatically. Styles currently apply in English only.Snippets (Android): Create text expansion shortcuts so a short trigger word automatically expands into a longer phrase or sentence. Manage snippets from a dedicated screen in the app with search and filtering.The Hub: Your home base for dictation stats, transcription history, dictionary, snippets, and scratchpad. The header bar shows your daily streak, total words dictated, and average words per minute. As you build your streak past 31 days, the Hub displays personalized milestone messages at key intervals. Word count stats are compared to familiar reference points like thank-you notes, news articles, and short film scripts.Getting startedDownload and installMobile: Download from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android). You can also scan the QR code in the Wispr Flow Hub or request a download link texted to your phone.Note: iOS 18.3 or later is required.Desktop: Go to wisprflow.ai/downloads and download Flow for Windows or Mac. Direct download links: Mac (Apple Silicon), Mac (Intel), Windows.Mac system requirementsAny Mac with Apple Silicon or Intel, macOS 12.0 or newer500 MB free storageBuilt-in or external microphoneInternet connectionWindows system requirementsWindows 10 (64-bit) or laterIntel Core i3 / AMD Ryzen 3 or better4 GB RAM (8 GB recommended)500 MB free storageBuilt-in or external microphoneInternet connectionCreate an accountSign in with Google, Apple, or Microsoft SSO, or create an account with email. If your organization uses SSO, you must sign in through SSO — email/password account creation is not available. If your organization uses directory sync (SCIM), contact your IT admin to get access.Enter your email address, full name, and a password (at least 6 characters). Your password cannot be the same as your full name.Complete the CAPTCHA verification if prompted.Click the verification link sent to your inbox from support@wispr.ai. Check your Spam/Trash folder if you don't see it.Start using Flow once verified.Note: If you forget your password, reset it via email. If your email is already linked to a Google, Apple, or Microsoft account, Flow prompts you to sign in with that provider. Email aliases with "+" (e.g., user+alias@example.com) are not supported.ExamplesDictating a message in any text fieldYou do: Place your cursor in a Slack message, press the hotkey, and say "Hey team, the design review is moved to 3pm tomorrow."Flow types: Hey team, the design review is moved to 3pm tomorrow.Using an AI command to edit textYou say: "Make this more professional."Flow rewrites: The selected text is rephrased in a more polished, professional tone.Dictating a note on iPhoneYou do: Tap the Wispr Flow widget on your lock screen, then tap the microphone button and speak.Flow does: Transcribes your words into a new note, which you can save or continue editing.Creating a note with SiriYou say to Siri: "Save Flow note" and then dictate your note content.Flow does: Captures your dictation as a note without you ever opening the app.FAQsWhat's the default hotkey to start dictating?Mac: Press the fn key (or Ctrl + Option if your keyboard lacks fn). Windows: Press Ctrl + Win.Can I use Flow offline?Flow requires an internet connection for voice transcription.Is enterprise SSO supported?Yes. Enter your work email address during sign-in and Flow identifies your organization's identity provider automatically.Do Personalization Styles sync across devices?Yes. The writing styles you set on Android sync to your account automatically, so your preferences are always up to date.How do I access Notes on iPhone?Open Notes directly in the Flow app, tap the lock screen or Control Center widget, or use Siri shortcuts like "Create note with Flow" or "Save Flow note." Notes are also searchable from the iOS search screen.Limitations and notesMac: Apple Silicon and Intel supported. macOS 12.0 or newer required.Windows: Windows 10 (64-bit) or later required.iPhone: iOS 18.3 or later required. Notes, lock screen widget, Control Center widget, Siri shortcuts, and Spotlight search are available on iOS.Android: Currently in Beta. Snippets and Personalization Styles are available on Android. Personalization Styles apply in English only. Some features may have limited functionality compared to desktop and iOS.Coming soon: Apple Watch, iPad, Linux.Want to prioritize your device? Email support@wispr.ai and tell us what you're most excited to do with Flow.
