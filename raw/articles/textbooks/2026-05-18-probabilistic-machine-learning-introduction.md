## Key Claims

- Probabilistic modeling provides a unifying language for prediction, uncertainty, missing data, latent variables, and learning.
- Supervised and unsupervised learning can be organized around probabilistic assumptions and objectives.
- Bayesian methods make uncertainty over parameters and latent structure explicit.
- Modern ML combines probabilistic foundations with neural representation learning.
