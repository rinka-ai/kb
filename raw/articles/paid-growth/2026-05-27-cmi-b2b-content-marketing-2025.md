# Content Marketing Institute: B2B Content Marketing Benchmarks, Budgets, and Trends - Outlook for 2025

## TL;DR

B2B content teams are under pressure to do more with limited resources, unclear goals, and immature creation models. Paid distribution is common, but content strategy still depends on clarity, repeatability, and human creativity.

## Key Claims

- Many B2B marketers rate their content strategy as only moderately effective.
- Common weaknesses include unclear goals, lack of scalable creation models, and resource constraints.
- Paid channels are widely used for content distribution, especially social advertising, SEM/PPC, display, and sponsorships.
- CMI warns against offloading creativity to AI rather than using AI to automate low-value work.

## Important Details

- The report surveyed 980 B2B marketers for the 2025 outlook.
- It reports broad use of paid channels, with social advertising/promoted posts and SEM/PPC among prominent channels.
- It distinguishes output volume from strategic effectiveness.
- The AI lesson is not "avoid AI," but "do not automate away the creative judgment that gives content value."
