# New Research Reveals Sales Reps Need a Productivity Overhaul

## TL;DR

This source supports the sales-ops baseline problem: sales productivity is constrained by admin, deal management, data entry, and tool burden. It does not by itself prove that AI has failed to improve sales, but it helps explain why naive AI wrappers may not change the real bottleneck.

## Key Claims

- Salesforce surveyed more than 7,700 sales professionals across 38 countries.
- Reps spent a minority of their week actually selling.
- Administrative work, deal management, and data entry consumed much of the remaining time.
- Many sales organizations planned to consolidate tech stacks because tool overload was a productivity issue.

## Important Details

- This is an earlier baseline source from before the current agent wave.
- It is useful when comparing AI promises against longstanding workflow friction in sales organizations.
- The source comes from a CRM vendor, so use the exact metric carefully and prefer independent corroboration when making strong claims.
