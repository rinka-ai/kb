## TL;DR

Meta's current performance advice is not "micro-target harder." It is to simplify campaign structure so the delivery system can learn, use automation, diversify creative, improve signal quality, and validate results through testing.

## Key Claims

- Meta positions Performance 5 as a five-part operating model for advertisers using AI-enabled delivery.
- Account simplification reduces time spent in the learning phase and gives the system clearer budget and signal paths.
- Creative diversification is a core performance lever because the system needs multiple messages and formats to match people and placements.
- First-party data and Conversions API improve optimization and measurement when browser-side tracking is degraded.
- Results validation is necessary because platform-reported performance still needs testing discipline.

## Important Details

- The source emphasizes combining ad sets and minimizing disruptive edits while campaigns are learning.
- Creative diversification means developing a variety of ad creative and letting Meta deliver the relevant message to the relevant person.
- Data quality is framed as a direct connection between marketing data and Meta technologies, especially through Conversions API.
- Results validation appears as one of the five pillars, which aligns with incrementality and lift-study discipline rather than dashboard-only attribution.
- The guidance implicitly favors fewer, cleaner campaigns with more creative inputs over fragmented campaign structures.
