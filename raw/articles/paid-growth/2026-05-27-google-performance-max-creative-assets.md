# Google Ads: Best practices for Performance Max creative assets

## Source Metadata

- Path: raw/articles/paid-growth/2026-05-27-google-performance-max-creative-assets.md

- Publisher: Google Ads Help

## TL;DR

Performance Max needs a broad and refreshed asset library. Google recommends adding many text, image, video, business, and call-to-action assets so the system can assemble more relevant combinations across channels.

## Key Claims

- Creative assets are essential to Performance Max campaign performance.
- Advertisers should refresh assets as offers, products, seasons, and messages change.
- More assets create more ad combinations across Google's inventory.
- Low-rated assets should not simply be deleted unless replacement assets are ready.

## Important Details

- Google recommends at least seven image assets, including a 1200 x 1200 square asset.
- Assets can include location, affiliate location, callout, call, sitelink, structured snippet, lead form, and price assets depending on goals.
- Calls to action should align with the campaign goal.
- Business or brand name should appear in ad text.
- For time-bound promotions, Google recommends planning and refreshing creative ahead of the event rather than abruptly changing everything at the last minute.
