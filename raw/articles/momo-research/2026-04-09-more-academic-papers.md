## TL;DR

This note is a secondary reading list of more theoretical or exploratory papers around retrieval limits, test-time memorization, nested learning, and episodic reuse.

## Key Claims

- Single-vector embedding retrieval has structural limitations for complex relevance tasks.
- Test-time memorization modules remain an active area of architectural innovation.
- Episodic or latent reuse mechanisms may complement parametric learning in useful ways.

## Important Details

- The note includes papers on embedding retrieval limits, Titans, Nested Learning, and latent learning with episodic memory.
- The author frames these as significant but somewhat less directly tied to practical memory system design.
- This makes the note better as a backlog or frontier-reading source than as a core implementation guide.
