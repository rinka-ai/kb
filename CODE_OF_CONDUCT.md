# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want this repository to be welcoming, thoughtful, and respectful.

Examples of positive behavior:

- being constructive and kind in reviews and discussions
- focusing on evidence, clarity, and collaboration
- respecting different levels of expertise
- helping others learn and contribute successfully

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- personal attacks or bad-faith argument
- doxxing or publishing private information
- intentionally disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit content, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately through the repository's supported channels.
