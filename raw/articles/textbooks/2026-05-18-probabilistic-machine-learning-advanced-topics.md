## TL;DR

A large advanced ML reference that connects probabilistic inference with modern deep learning, causality, generative modeling, sequential decisions, and system-scale concerns.

## Key Claims

- Advanced ML often reduces to managing uncertainty over latent variables, parameters, functions, causal structure, and sequential decisions.
- Approximate inference is central because exact probabilistic reasoning is often computationally infeasible.
- Deep models and probabilistic models are complementary rather than separate traditions.
- Causality, fairness, robustness, and deployment concerns belong inside the modern probabilistic ML map.
