## TL;DR

Google Conversion Lift is the official reminder that attributed conversions are not the same thing as incremental conversions. It uses controlled exposed and holdback groups to estimate causal impact.

## Key Claims

- Conversion lift measures incremental conversions driven by Google Ads campaigns.
- It separates audiences into people who receive ads and people who are held back from receiving ads.
- The difference in downstream conversions estimates lift caused by ads.
- Results can include incremental conversions, conversion value, cost, and incremental ROAS depending on study type.

## Important Details

- Google supports user-based and geography-based lift approaches in eligible accounts.
- Conversion lift is not available for all Google Ads accounts.
- Experiment duration should account for conversion lag; short studies can understate lift for longer purchase cycles.
- Holdbacks have an opportunity cost because some eligible users intentionally do not see ads.
