## TL;DR

The screenshot frames AI-agency Meta ads as a simple, direct-response format: call out the target niche immediately, make the AI mechanism obvious, sell appointment/result outcomes, use high-contrast niche-specific creative, and use headline/description fields to reinforce market specificity and trust.

## Key Claims

- Meta ad primary text should identify the target market before the "See More" cutoff.
- The word "NEW" is recommended as a freshness cue.
- Copy and image text should be written at a 5th-grade reading level.
- AI-related wording and symbols should make the mechanism obvious to non-technical buyers.
- Appointment booking, lower workload, follow-up handling, ROI, and success stories are positioned as the core AI-agency outcomes.
- Creative should use niche-specific imagery, strong contrast, and simple overlay text.
- Headlines should directly name the market and make the offer feel built for that market.
- Descriptions should reinforce the ad's main message and add proof or trust.

## Important Details

- The checklist is tactical and practitioner-oriented, not platform policy guidance.
- "ChatGPT Plugin" should only be used if the offer actually depends on a ChatGPT plugin or equivalent integration; otherwise the claim can become misleading.
- ROI, appointment, and success-story claims need substantiation before use in live ads.
- Target-market call-outs are useful, but Meta copy must avoid implying sensitive personal attributes.
- The visual guidance favors static-style high-contrast direct-response creatives: black/white or white/red palettes, colored borders around text, and niche symbols that make the audience legible at a glance.

Key Elements of Winning Meta Ad Copy For AI Agency Ads

- Call out who the ad is for at the very top, before the "See More" cutoff
- Use the word "NEW" to signal it's fresh, modern, and cutting-edge
- Write at a 5th-grade reading level - keep it simple, no big or confusing words
- Use the words "A.I." and "ChatGPT Plugin" often so people understand it's AI-powered
- Lead with results - mention success stories if you have them; if not, focus on how new the system is and the strong ROI it can deliver
- Emphasize that your system books more appointments with less work, and that the A.I. handles follow-up for them

Key Elements of Winning Meta Ad Creative For AI Agency Ads

- Use text on the image to call out the target market (e.g. "GYM OWNERS" or "NAIL SALONS")
- Use the word "NEW" prominently alongside the ChatGPT Plugin
- Add niche-relevant symbols that match the target market (e.g. Medspa = Cryo chamber, Nail Salon = painted nails)
- Include robot or tech-looking symbols to reinforce the AI angle
- Use strong contrast - light words on dark background or vice versa
- Stick to black/white or white/red color themes for bold contrast
- Add colored borders around text that differ from both the text and background for a triple-layer contrast effect
- Avoid bright backgrounds like yellow or blue - stick to white or dark
- Keep image copy at a 5th-grade reading level - run it through Hemingway Editor to confirm

Key Elements of Winning Meta Ad Headline For AI Agency Ads

- Use the word "New"
- Call out the target market directly
- Use "!" or an emoji to communicate urgency at the end
- Make it clear the offer is specifically built for their market

Key Elements of Winning Meta Ad Description For AI Agency Ads (Social Proof/Trust Layer)

- Use it to reinforce the main message of the ad
- Show proof, build trust, and make the offer feel stronger
