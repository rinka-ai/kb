# Meta Advantage+ Creative

## TL;DR

Meta Advantage+ creative turns ad production into an asset-variation problem. The advertiser supplies brand, offer, and creative direction; Meta's AI adapts media, text, and audio across audiences and placements.

## Key Claims

- Advantage+ creative can generate and optimize ad images, videos, text, and audio for different placements and audiences.
- Meta presents creative diversification as both a performance lever and a resource-efficiency lever.
- Text generation uses supplied primary text and headlines to create variations that reflect brand voice and selling points.
- Image expansion, background generation, animation, and music generation are meant to make creative fit more placements.

## Important Details

- Meta says some enhancements may be turned on when the system predicts they will improve performance.
- The setup flow asks advertisers to add multiple text options and preview variations before publishing.
- Advantage+ placements are recommended for broader placement coverage.
- The product creates a new responsibility: teams need brand and compliance guardrails for AI-generated ad variations.
