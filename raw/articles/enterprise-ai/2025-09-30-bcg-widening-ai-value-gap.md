# Are You Generating Value from AI? The Widening Gap

## TL;DR

BCG corroborates the enterprise AI value gap, but frames it as a maturity gap rather than a universal failure story: a small future-built cohort gets outsized results, while many firms remain stuck with minimal revenue or cost impact.

## Key Claims

- BCG identifies a small share of firms as AI future-built and a much larger lagging group seeing little material value.
- Future-built companies reportedly generate substantially better revenue increases and cost reductions from AI than other companies.
- Agentic AI is becoming part of the value gap, with leading companies allocating more AI budget to agents and adopting them sooner.
- BCG presents a playbook for the other firms to build maturity rather than waiting for better models.

## Important Details

- The report is useful because it separates AI spending from AI maturity.
- It treats value creation as compounding through reinvestment, stronger people capabilities, and stronger technology capabilities.
- It supports the thesis that organizational capability and operating model matter as much as model access.
