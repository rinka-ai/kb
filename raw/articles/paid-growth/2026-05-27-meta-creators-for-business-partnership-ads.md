# Meta: Creators for Business and Partnership Ads

## TL;DR

Meta treats creators as campaign infrastructure, not only organic influencer talent. Creator marketplace helps find creators, while partnership ads let brands run creator-linked content as paid ads with permissioning and measurement inside Meta.

## Key Claims

- Creators help brands tap credibility, trends, and audience trust.
- Partnership ads can integrate creator handles and content into always-on paid strategies.
- Meta claims partnership ads can improve acquisition cost, brand lift, clickthrough, and cost per ad in tested contexts.
- The Partnership Ads Hub centralizes content permissions and eligibility troubleshooting.

## Important Details

- Partnership ads are available across multiple formats and placements.
- Meta says partnership ads can be used with Advantage+ creative and audio swap.
- The source ties creator content to full-funnel objectives, from awareness to sales.
- The operational constraint is permissioning: creator content must be made available to the brand before it can be promoted.
