## TL;DR

A fairness text that keeps the KB from reducing safety to technical metrics: legitimacy, measurement, human agency, causality, feedback, and institutional context are part of ML system design.

## Key Claims

- Fairness cannot be solved by choosing one statistical criterion in isolation.
- Measurement choices and target-goal mismatches can encode social failures before modeling starts.
- Common criteria such as independence, separation, and sufficiency can conflict.
- Automated decisions create feedback loops, recourse problems, and legitimacy questions that are broader than classifier accuracy.
