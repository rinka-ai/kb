## TL;DR

A validation and assurance text that expands eval thinking beyond benchmark scores into property specification, falsification, stochastic system modeling, and safety evidence.

## Key Claims

- Validation starts with system models and explicit properties, not only empirical scoreboards.
- Stochastic systems need metrics and specifications that account for uncertainty.
- Falsification can be framed as an optimization problem over disturbances and failure-inducing inputs.
- Safety-critical AI needs evidence across modeling, testing, reachability, and robustness, not only average-case performance.
