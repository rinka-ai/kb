# Meta: Instagram and Facebook Reels Ads

## TL;DR

Reels ads are a separate creative environment, not just another placement. Meta emphasizes vertical 9:16 video, audio, safe-zone discipline, native creative, creator workflows, and A/B testing to understand placement impact.

## Key Claims

- Reels is built into Meta Advantage and can be selected through Advantage+ placements or manual placement settings.
- Creative built for Reels should respect vertical format, audio, and safe-zone requirements.
- Meta recommends testing native Reels creative or Reels-optimized assets to understand performance.
- Creators and partners can help produce native Reels creative at speed.

## Important Details

- Meta says Reels defaults to sound-on when the user has not disabled sound.
- Existing reels can be promoted, including branded content if the creator permits business promotion.
- Advantage+ creative can adapt aspect ratio and add music for Reels.
- Meta cites internal split-test meta-analyses where 9:16 video with audio and safe-zone discipline outperformed image-based Reels ads.
