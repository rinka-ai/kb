# Obsidian

## Summary

Obsidian is used here as the human-facing frontend for browsing raw sources, compiled concept pages, and generated artifacts like slides or plots.

## Why It Fits

- local markdown-first workflow
- wiki-link navigation
- good support for mixed notes and derived artifacts
- useful as the browsing layer even when indexing and synthesis happen outside Obsidian itself
